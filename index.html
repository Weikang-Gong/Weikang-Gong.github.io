Brief Biography

From 2015, Mr. Weikang Gong acted as a master student of Faculty of Environmental and Life Sciences in Beijing University of Technology (BJUT) and a member of Bioinformatics Lab, led by Prof. Dr. Chunhua Li. He entered in the Ph.D study phase in 2017. He is currently a Ph.D student. From 2021 to 2022, he acted as a visiting student of School of Physical and Mathematical Sciences in the Nanyang Technological University (NTU) in Singapore, funded by China Scholarship Council (CSC) and led by Prof. Dr. Kelin Xia. He is interested in the following topics:

  • Protein/RNA/Protein-RNA Complex Structure-Dynamics Relationship

  • Protein-Protein Interaction Network and Complex Structure Prediction

  • Protein-RNA Recognition and Interaction
